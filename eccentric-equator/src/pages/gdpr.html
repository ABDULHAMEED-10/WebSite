GDPR Policy

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract fulfillment (providing taxi services)
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Consent (for marketing communications)

Data Protection Measures

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Regular security assessments
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular data backup and recovery testing

International Data Transfers

Your data is stored and processed within the United Kingdom. Any transfers outside the UK comply with UK GDPR requirements.

Data Retention

We retain your data for:

  • Journey details: 2 years
  • Payment information: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Marketing preferences: Until consent withdrawal

Your GDPR Rights

  • Right to be informed
  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Rights related to automated decision-making
