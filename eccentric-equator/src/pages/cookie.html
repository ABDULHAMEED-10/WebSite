Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

  • Session cookies for booking functionality
  • Security cookies
  • Load balancing cookies

Performance Cookies

  • Analytics cookies to understand usage
  • Error monitoring cookies

Functionality Cookies

  • Remember your preferences
  • Store location information

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics (performance measurement)
  • Payment processors
  • Booking system cookies

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. To opt-out of specific cookies:

  1. Open your browser settings
  2. Look for the privacy/cookie settings
  3. Select your preferred cookie options

Note: Blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience of our site.
